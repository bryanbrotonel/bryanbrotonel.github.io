When In New West

Fyndit is an Android application developed by two classmates of mine. The purpose of Fyndit is to support local business around the city of New Westminster.

Fyndit allows users to explore the New Westminster through different types of activities. Ranging from sports activities to exploring the diverse cuisine, Fyndit allows you, as the name suggests, find ways to explore the city, whatever it may be.

Toolset

  1. JAVA
  2. JSON
  3. GOOGLE SEARCH API

Fydnit in its entirety was programmed in Java, using Android Studio as our IDE of choice. JSON was chosen to serve as our small back end to store all the necessary data for the app. The Google Search API was integrated into the app for something we will talk about later.
